project scope

  • Client: Shing Yoga
  • Project: Mobile app
  • Language: Mandarin
  • Role: Sole designer
  • Key tasks:
    • User research
    • Persona
    • Rough screens (lo-fi wireframes)
    • Final screens (hi-fi wireframes)
    • Prototypes
  • Duration: 2 months (July 2018)

Shing Yoga is a membership only yoga studio based in Taipei, Taiwan. The members can reserve and cancel classes through an online system, and they receive information via Facebook or printed notices around the studio.

As the business is growing rapidly with over 800 active members, it seeks to improve members’ experience by providing a more effective and integrated platform for class reservations and communications. This will replace the current tools and become the primary method for studio members to check class schedules, reserve classes, receive alerts, create favorites, and store fitness progresses.

user research

To start the process, I began conducting user research to understand the goals and motivations of the target audience. I recruited users from social media and yoga studio sales representatives. During the interviews, I was particularly interested in their daily routine, priorities & goals, active lifestyle, studio experience, and reservation experience. From the results, I used affinity mapping to synthesize the research and derived a persona, concluded with a problem statement, and suggested a solution.

rough screens

After analyzing the findings, I created a set of lo-fi wireframes that would be part of the member’s user experience through the platform.

style guide

I picked the font PingFang TC for its clarity and simplicity so members can quickly navigate through the interface. The main color is a pink color specific to the brand with gray and black to match the clean interface.

final screens

Upon confirming the features and visual directions with the client, I designed the final screens according to the style guide.
